Super Pew Pew

Github Repository
    Controls:
  • WASD to move
  • Left click to shoot
  • Space to jump (hold for larger jump)

Instructions:
Collect as many weapon crates (rainbow squares) as you can before succumbing to the endless hordes of relentless squares. Make full use of the weapons provided to you by the weapon crates, as without them you stand little chance. Let enemies reach the bottom of the stage at your own risk, as they will become enraged, increasing in speed and ferocity o_O.

Description:
Super Pew Pew is an HTML5 game made (mostly) from scratch. It is designed using a custom entity-component-system (ECS) programmed from the ground up by me. This system facilitates the fundamental behaviours necessary for most games, such as physics simulation, mouse interaction, layered rendering, event management, etc.

The game utilises the p2.js library (made by Stefan Hedman) for physics simulation, and uses the font '8-bit Arcade' created by Damien Gosset.
